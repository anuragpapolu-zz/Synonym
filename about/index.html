Cinnamon

Cinnamon was created at HackGenY 2015 by Anurag, Hari, Zuhayer, and Srinath in order to help writers improve their essays by identifying words that could be replaced by better ones.

Try it out!

Synonym Identifier

Identifies words that can be replaced, and offers suggestions with the click of a button.

Plagarism Checker

Our service not only identifies replacable words, it also checks online sources to see if any work is plagarized.

And Totally Free

Cinnamon is free and offers user privacy, as it does not store any work edited on the site.

Cinnamon's Story

Cinnamon was born when Srinath struggled with his diction in essays, as he was unable find the correct words to express himself. Zuhayer thought it would be much more convenient to have a program that can identify synonyms for multiple words at once with the click of a button.

Unique Features

Synonym Identifier

Identifies words that can be replaced with synonyms and provides suggestions.

Plagarism Checker

Checks online sources to make sure that all work is unique.

Word Count

See your essay statistics.

File Upload

Get access to your Dropbox, iCloud, Google Drive, or import a file from your computer.
